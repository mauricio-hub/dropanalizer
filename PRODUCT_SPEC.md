# Proply — Product Spec

## What is Proply

Proply is a SaaS platform that lets freelancers and small agencies create, send, and track commercial proposals as dynamic landing pages.

Instead of sending a PDF or a Word doc, users send a link. The client opens a polished landing page. The user sees exactly how the client engaged with it.

---

## Who it's for

- Freelancers (designers, developers, consultants)
- Small agencies
- Anyone who sends commercial proposals and wants to close deals faster

---

## Core Problem

Proposals sent as PDFs are static, untrackable, and hard to iterate on.

Proply solves:
- **No visibility** — you don't know if the client read your proposal
- **Slow iteration** — editing a PDF and resending is painful
- **Unprofessional format** — a landing page looks better than an attachment

---

## MVP Scope (strict)

1. **Create a proposal** — title + free-form brief, service mode only
2. **Save to database** — proposal persisted per authenticated user
3. **Dashboard** — list of user's proposals

---

## Explicitly NOT in MVP

- Public landing page / shareable URL
- Tracking / events
- Versioning
- AI content generation
- Product catalog
- Analytics dashboard
- Email notifications
- Custom domains
- Teams / multi-user workspaces
- Payment integration
- PDF export
